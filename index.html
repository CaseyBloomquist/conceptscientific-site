What We Do

Custom Research Tools & Instrumentation

When the instrument you need doesn't exist, we design and build it. From electrochemical reactors to sensor arrays and automated test rigs — purpose-built for your experiment.

Rapid Prototyping

Concept to physical prototype, fast. We work across 3D printing, CNC machining, electronics, and mechanical fabrication to get something in your hands quickly — built to learn from.

Systems Integration

Sensors, controls, fluidics, software, and hardware into a single working system. We connect the pieces and make the whole thing run — reliably, in a research environment.

Research & Technical Consulting

Not sure where to start? We scope problems, sketch technical approaches, and help research teams pressure-test ideas before committing resources. Useful whether or not you build with us.
